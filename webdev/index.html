What we do

FarmSense makes agriculture smarter for all stakeholders

Objective:

Establish a web portal that provides access to open data and information on farms and farmers activities in Akure (North/South) and Ifedore LGAs of Ondo state

Governments

Increased access of the government and other Agricultural stakeholders to 'open data' on farms and farmers information in Akure (North/South) and Okitupupa LGAs of Ondo state.

Academia / Innovators

Reduced frustration in accessing data on farms and farmers information in Akure by the government and Agricultural stakeholders for planning and decison making purposes.

Agri-business & Investors

Improved the quality of road data and POI. To help facilitate decision making.
